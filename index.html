How does account access work?

When you click Connect in the extension, it opens the marketplace's own login page (eBay, Grailed, etc.) in your browser. Your login cookies are cached locally on your device — we never transmit or store your credentials on our servers. Even if our servers were compromised, there's nothing to steal. A malicious actor would need physical access to your device to do anything with your session.
