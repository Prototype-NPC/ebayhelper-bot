What is EbayHelper?

EbayHelper (@Ebayhelper111bot) is a Telegram bot that lets you sell your clothing on eBay Canada without needing your own eBay account, without dealing with listings, shipping policies, or payment setup — and without ever leaving Telegram.

You send photos of your item. Our AI reads the photos, writes the full listing for you, and posts it live on eBay Canada in under 2 minutes. When it sells, you get paid your cut automatically.

Think of it like a consignment store, except it's on eBay, it's automated, and you keep 77% of every sale.

help

Why We Use Our eBay Account (Not Yours)

When you sell on eBay, the platform requires a verified seller account with an established history, connected payment method, shipping policies, and return policies already configured. Setting all of this up from scratch takes days, and new accounts get limited until they build trust with eBay.

Our account already has all of this in place — verified seller status, shipping policies, payment processing, and a transaction history. When your item is listed through us, it benefits from that credibility immediately. Your listing goes live faster, gets better placement in search results, and buyers trust it more than a brand new account with zero history.

This also means you never have to deal with eBay's seller interface, policy compliance, or account management. You just send photos and collect your money.

How the Fees Work

Every sale is split three ways:

eBay — platform fee, charged to all sellers
~13%
Us — account management, listings, disputes
10%
You keep
~77%

Example: Your hoodie sells for $100 → eBay takes $13, we take $10, you receive $77.

There are no listing fees, no upfront costs, and no charge if the item doesn't sell. You only pay when it sells.

How to Create a Listing

The full listing flow, step by step:

Step 1 — Start a new listing

Send /list or /start to begin.

Step 2 — Send your photos

Send up to 10 photos of your item. Include:

  • Front, back, and side views
  • Size tag, brand tag, material tag
  • Any flaws or damage (be honest!)

Step 3 — Finish uploading

Send /done when all photos are uploaded.

Step 4 — Review AI suggestions

AI analyzes your photos and suggests:

Title Description Brand Category Style Department Color Size Material

Step 5 — Approve or override

Happy with everything? Send /approve to accept all fields.

Want to change something? Use override commands:

/title /brand /size /color /category /description

Step 6 — Set your price

Enter your asking price. The AI will suggest one based on eBay sold data, but you can set whatever you want.

Step 7 — Condition & offers

Choose the item condition:

New Like New Good Acceptable

Then choose whether to accept offers from buyers and set your minimum acceptable price.

Step 8 — Promote (optional)

Optionally promote your listing with eBay Ads. You choose a percentage — the higher the %, the more visibility. You only pay the promotion fee if the item actually sells.

check_circle Step 9 — Confirm & go live

Review everything one last time. Hit confirm and your listing goes live on eBay Canada instantly.

Managing Your Listings

Use /mylistings to see and manage all your active listings. From there you can:

  • visibility View all active listings with price and status
  • edit Edit the title or price on any listing
  • cancel End a listing early
  • campaign Promote a listing for more visibility
  • sell View sold listings and your earnings

Getting Notified

The bot keeps you in the loop via Telegram. You'll get notified when:

  • sell An item sells — with sale price and your payout amount
  • local_offer A buyer makes an offer — with the offer amount
  • chat A buyer sends a message — questions about your item

info Note: Replies to offers and buyer messages must be handled directly on eBay, as the platform doesn't allow third-party responses through the API.

FAQ

How long does it take to list an item?

About 30 seconds once you've taken photos. Send them, wait for AI, approve, done. You can list 6 items in the time it takes to manually list 1 on eBay.

What if the AI gets something wrong?

No worries — you always review before anything goes live. Use override commands like /title, /brand, or /size to correct any field before approving.

Can I list non-clothing items?

Right now, EbayHelper is optimized for clothing and apparel. We may expand to other categories in the future.

When do I get paid?

After your item sells and the buyer receives it, your payout is calculated (sale price minus eBay fees and our 10%). Payouts are arranged directly with the admin.

What if my item doesn't sell?

No fees are charged unless your item sells. You can edit the price, promote the listing, or end it at any time using /mylistings.

Why can't I use my own eBay account?

Platforms like eBay require developer API authentication (OAuth credentials, app registration, API keys) to post listings programmatically. This process takes weeks and isn't available to most sellers. Our pre-authorized account lets you skip all of that.
